Colin Eberhardt has published an article on codeproject which describes the re-implementation of a Silverlight control for Windows Phone 7 using HTML5 / CSS. The re-implemented control enables it to be used across multiple mobile devices including iPhone, Android and modern BlackBerry phones.

This article goes beyond the differences in the technologies, to look at what it 'feels' like to move from C# to JavaScript.
